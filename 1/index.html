Supporting MATTERS


What should I do if I need a settlement letter ? Contact us on 15022 after repaying your loan and request a settlement letter. It will be issued within a month from your request date. What can I do if I forgot my pin or lost my phone? Click on forget pin and you will start the process to create a new one, or contact us on 15022 For assistance. Can I use my existing Kashat account with a new phone Device ? Yes, you can use your existing Kashat account with a new phone. Just login on your Kashat account on your new phone and it will be automatically updated.
